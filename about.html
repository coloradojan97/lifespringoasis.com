About Jan DeCourtney

Jan DeCourtney is a Certified Massage Therapist, co-author with Walt Stoll, MD of the book, Recapture Your Health and past Director of the Stoll Foundation for Holistic Health. She has lived in Boulder since 1997 and in this condo since 2000. She has traveled widely and enjoys meeting new people.

As your hostess at Life Spring Oasis Personal Retreat, Jan strives to support you in accomplishing your work or study goals that bring you to Boulder.
