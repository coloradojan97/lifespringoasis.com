Life Spring Oasis Features

Your Personal Retreat for Healing, Transformation, and Creativity

   

Your room includes a comfortable full-size mattress with cotton sheets and natural fabric bedding (cotton quilts, cotton and wool blankets), and a choice of natural or hypoallergenic pillows. Blackout blinds block artificial outdoor light at night and early morning sunshine if you choose. Your desk enables you to take advantage of our high speed wireless internet access.

Your adjacent bathroom features a full bathtub and a shower. You will also find a blow dryer, cotton towels, natural hand soap, and a rubber bath mat.

Indulge your inner chef by storing food in your room's mini-refrig/mini-freezer, and by heating foods in your room's personal microwave. Or, make use of the condo's kitchen, including a stove, oven, large refrigerator, dishwasher and privileges for using spices and condiments. Available for your use is a juicer, steamer, crock pot, coffee maker, and other cooking appliances and utensils. In the kitchen area you will also find purified water for drinking, an electric tea kettle for making tea, and a washer and dryer.

The comfortable condo has a vaulted ceiling, skylights, and a working wood stove. In the living room you will find a large screen TV for live streaming (and VCR and DVD players with a movie library). Relaxation CDs are also available for listening.

You may also enjoy sitting on the balcony, which overlooks a beautiful and peaceful old growth recreational park. Gunbarrel Commons Park has hiking trails to streams and lakes, glimpses of the mountains, a workout course, and basketball court. The trails and streets in this quiet neighborhood are great for walking and cycling.


click to view larger image

Our condo complex offers a year-round outdoor hot tub and summer swimming pool. The park across the street offers a workout course. Trails are nearby at the park and at the Twin Lakes Open Space area.

Other Special Features:

  • Parking: Off-street parking in safe neighborhood
  • Transportation: Two blocks away from bus stops (J and 205 routes).
  • Shops: walking or biking distance (about 8 blocks) to shopping area with groceries, restaurants, fitness center/gym, brewery and factory tours. See the "Just for You" section!

Services customized to your unique needs by advance arrangement for additional fee

  • massage therapy
  • skilled relaxation instruction
  • laundry service
  • herbal or Epsom salt baths
  • business, personal assistant, or secretarial services
  • courier/chauffer services
