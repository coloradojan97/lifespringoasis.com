For Students

Gunbarrel is home to several wonderful schools of natural healing: Boulder College of Massage Therapy, the Rolf Institute, and Southwest Acupuncture College.

Life Spring Oasis provides a quiet and comfortable atmosphere for you to excel in your studies. Your room comes complete with a computer desk and 12 bps wifi, plus a mini-refrigerator and microwave for those late-night study sessions.

The Rolf Institute is about 7 blocks away and Southwest Acupuncture College about 8 blocks. You could walk, or easily ride a bike to get there. Let me know if you need someone to practice on!
