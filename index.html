Welcome to Life Spring Oasis!

Your Home Away From Home....
Comfortable Room for Rent...
Women only!

I extend a warm invitation for you to stay in my comfortable, cozy room for rent in my quiet Boulder condo. You'll find this a warm and inviting place to stay where you can focus and excel at any work or study situation that brings you to Boulder. Your room is waiting for you.

Location:

Boulder, Gunbarrel, Front Range, Colorado, USA

Accommodations:

One private bedroom with full-size bed and full bath, shared living room, dining room, kitchen, and balcony in beautiful Willowbrook Condos in the Gunbarrel neighborhood of Boulder, Colorado.

The room is available for short or long-term stays from 1 night to 1 year.
click to view larger image

Ideal for:

  • Rolfing, acupuncture, or massage students
  • Executives, scientists or consultants, needing temporary housing for their visit
  • Athletes participating in sporting events such as Bolder Boulder or Boulder Marathon
  • Women (sorry guys, my place is just too small)

This warm and inviting cozy condo features a vaulted ceiling, skylights, a wood stove, and many other special amenities. The subtle colors of the decor are warm and soothing, and you can watch the organic container garden grow veggies in the summer! One sweet indoor tabby cat who has free roam of his house will welcome you. Whether you are visiting temporarily for work or study, or participating in one of Boulder's exciting athletic events, Life Spring Oasis can help make your visit comfortable and comforting.

Owner description:

Jan is a massage therapist, certified nurse's aide, and holistic health educator. She loves to meet people. If you are in need of special services or help, she is delighted to assist you, as her passion is helping others excel in their lives and helping you enjoy your stay in Boulder. Jan and her cat look forward to welcoming you to our cozy home.

Suitability:

  • Second floor (no elevator)
  • No smoking
  • No pets
  • One cat lives in the unit with the owner
  • Accommodation is for 1 person, female
