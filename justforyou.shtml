Just for You

From a daylong wellness personal retreat to a year's stay while attending school, your comfort and happiness are important. This is my home and yours too during your stay. Make yourself at home with a cup of tea!

The room is available for most anyone staying in Boulder, including people needing housing while relocating to Boulder, here for vacation, visiting CU for homecoming, or visiting friends or family in Willowbrook, Powderhorn, Hunter Creek, or Habitat communities. I will do my best to customize your visit to meet your special needs whatever brings you to Boulder.

Special amenities available for executives and scientists, athletes and students.

Please let me know how I can help you and make you as comfortable as possible during your stay. Your time here, and my hospitality, is just for you!
