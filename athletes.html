Life Spring Oasis

For Visiting Athletes

Are you here for foot races such as the Bolder Boulder, the Frozen Foot 5K, the Boulder Spring Half, the Boulder 100 Endurance Race, or Boulder Marathon?

Ready for special cycling races such as the iconic Koppenberg Spring Classic, the infamous Mike Horgan Hill Climb, Superior Morgul, the Louisville Criterium, or the Boulder Cyclocross Series?

Are you participating in other events such as Rohto Ironman 70.3 Boulder Triathlon, the Muddy Buddy Race, or the IronGirl Triathlon?

Life Spring Oasis is a great place to stay, offering:

  • massage therapy services (I'm a certified massage therapist with 14 years experience)
  • a place to cook your own special food in my whole foods kitchen
  • a year-round hot tub
  • tennis courts
  • close proximity to the Boulder Reservoir where many local sporting events take place.

We can customize aspects of your stay to help your event go smoothly. Such as, I may be available to drive you to and from your event. Or, you can warm up by cycling or jogging to the Boulder Reservoir, which is only about 3.5 miles away. Let me know what you need to succeed!
